What is this report about? This report is about the accesibility of blackboard in terms of usability heuristics. 
Blackboard is a website used by schools and colleges to help improve college life for both lecturers/teachers and students.
On blackboard you can check many things. You can check Posts, Updates on events like returned assignments, Your marks in a module/subject, and a calender.
We will soon see all the positives and negatives and investigate this website in terms of usability and heuristics.
